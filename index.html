My grandparents met during their studies in the University of Fine Arts in Valencia. Most of my close relatives work in the field of visual arts.

During the last two years of my Arts studies, I concentrated on Contemporary Art, Film, and Photography. When I finished I wanted to specialize in Cinematography and decided to move to Barcelona to study a Masters in Cinematography in ESCAC (Superior School of Cinema of Catalonia). Since then I'm based in Barcelona and my work is focused mainly on Photography and Cinematography. I also teach workshops specialized in natural light and try to direct my work towards a more natural feel, creating atmospheres that recall the painters I’ve always admired.
